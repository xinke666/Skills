# Public Preview v0.1 / 公开预览版 v0.1

## 中文

`Fitness Coach RPG` 现已作为公开预览版发布。

这是一个可复用的 AI 健身教练框架，核心思路是把训练记录、恢复判断、成长反馈与可选剧情模式放进同一套本地文件结构中长期运行。

本次发布包含：

- 可复用的 `SKILL.md`
- 三类示例档案：`FITNESS / STORY / WORLD`
- 一个不绑定固定主线的通用叙事引擎
- 默认世界模板（可直接开档）
- 最小运行示例与作者设计参考

适合：

- 想长期记录训练并获得更连续反馈的人
- 喜欢 RPG 成长感和结构化训练叙事的人
- 想把 AI 用在本地个人系统而不只是聊天的人

你可以把它当作一个“可开新档、可持续进化”的训练框架：训练越长期，建议越贴合，角色与故事也会逐步长出来。

---

## English

`Fitness Coach RPG` is now available as a public preview.

It is a reusable AI fitness coach framework that combines workout logging, recovery-aware planning, progression feedback, and optional story mode in one local-file system.

This release includes:

- a reusable `SKILL.md`
- three example logs: `FITNESS / STORY / WORLD`
- a generic story engine without a fixed default storyline
- a ready-to-use default world template
- a minimal run example and author design references

Best for:

- people who want long-term workout tracking with continuity
- people who enjoy RPG progression and structured training narrative
- people who want to use AI as part of a local personal system

Think of it as a "new save file" framework: the longer you use it, the better it understands your training patterns, recovery rhythm, and growth direction.
